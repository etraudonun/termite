Device Detection Explained

In this page we provide a set of slides explaining the interaction between the different entities involved with Termite. We base our explanation on a demo application called PeerScanner which simply serves as a WiFi device scanner. The idea is to show the interaction the developer must have with the Termite client, as well as the results of said interactions.
